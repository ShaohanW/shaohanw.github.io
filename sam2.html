Overview / 项目简介

English

This project fine-tunes SAM2 with LoRA for brain tumor segmentation on BraTS MRI data. The original 3D NIfTI volumes are sliced into 2D images along the z-axis, and FLAIR-based inputs are used to adapt the model to medical segmentation tasks. During inference, a pseudo-video bidirectional propagation strategy is applied to generate continuous segmentation across the slice sequence.

中文

本项目基于 LoRA 对 SAM2 进行轻量化微调，用于 BraTS 脑肿瘤 MRI 数据分割。 原始三维 NIfTI 体数据沿 z 轴切分为二维图像，并采用基于 FLAIR 的输入形式 以适配医学图像分割任务。在推理阶段，使用伪视频双向传播策略，对切片序列进行连续分割。

Pipeline / 流程

English
  • Load BraTS 3D NIfTI MRI volumes with multi-modal scans.
  • Slice 3D volumes into 2D slices along the z-axis.
  • Construct model inputs from selected modalities and preprocess them into .npz files.
  • Apply LoRA to selected SAM2 Transformer layers for parameter-efficient fine-tuning.
  • Perform pseudo-video forward and backward propagation during inference.
中文
  • 读取 BraTS 多模态三维 NIfTI MRI 数据。
  • 沿 z 轴将三维体数据切分为二维切片。
  • 构建模型输入，并保存为 .npz 预处理文件。
  • 在 SAM2 的部分 Transformer 层中应用 LoRA，实现参数高效微调。
  • 在推理阶段进行伪视频前向与后向传播。

Method Highlights / 方法亮点

English
  • 3D MRI volumes are converted into 2D slices because SAM2 is designed for 2D inputs.
  • Pre-slicing improves training efficiency and GPU utilization.
  • FLAIR is duplicated into 3 channels to match SAM2's input format while preserving tumor sensitivity.
  • LoRA rank 16 adapters are applied to attention and MLP layers, tuning only about 0.3% of parameters.
中文
  • 由于 SAM2 面向二维输入，因此将三维 MRI 体数据转换为二维切片。
  • 预切片策略提升了训练效率和 GPU 利用率。
  • 将 FLAIR 单模态复制为三通道输入，以适配 SAM2 结构并保留肿瘤敏感性。
  • 在注意力层和 MLP 层中引入秩为16的 LoRA 适配器，仅微调约0.3%的参数。

Results / 结果

English

Experiments showed that lower-resolution training significantly reduced training time, while fine-tuning remained necessary for acceptable segmentation quality. Direct prediction without fine-tuning led to unsatisfactory results.

中文

实验表明，低分辨率训练能够显著减少训练时间，但为了获得可接受的分割质量， 仍然需要进行微调。若不进行微调而直接预测，则结果不可接受。

Limitations / 局限性

English

The current model is specialized for brain tumor segmentation and still requires one manually labeled slice as the initial prompt. Future work may increase the proportion of trainable parameters and use higher-resolution full-size inputs for improved performance.

中文

当前模型主要针对脑肿瘤分割，且仍需要人工标注一个初始切片作为提示输入。 后续工作可以进一步提高可训练参数比例，并尝试使用更高分辨率的完整输入提升性能。
